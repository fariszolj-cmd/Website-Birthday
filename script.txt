Design a premium mobile-first birthday memory gallery that feels like an emotional cinematic experience, not a standard photo album.

The gallery contains hundreds of photos and videos from a birthday celebration. The goal is to make users feel like they are reliving the entire day.

Style:

* Modern, luxury, immersive, emotional
* Apple-level design quality
* Dark elegant background with subtle gradients and glassmorphism
* Smooth micro-interactions and fluid animations
* No cheesy birthday graphics, balloons, confetti, or childish elements
* Clean typography with strong visual hierarchy

Layout:

* Full-screen vertical experience optimized for phones
* Dynamic masonry layout mixed with large hero moments
* Photos and videos have varying sizes to create rhythm and surprise
* Important moments automatically appear larger
* Videos autoplay silently when visible
* Seamless scrolling with parallax effects

Features:

* "Story Chapters" such as Arrival, Friends, Cake, Party, Late Night Memories
* AI-generated memory highlights section at the top
* Interactive timeline users can scrub through
* Floating date/time indicators while scrolling
* Beautiful transitions between sections
* Video previews integrated naturally among photos
* Memory cards with short captions and reactions

Animations:

* Smooth fade-ins
* Depth and motion effects while scrolling
* Photos slightly tilt and react to touch
* Elegant fullscreen viewer with swipe navigation
* Premium motion design inspired by Apple Photos, Instagram, and modern award-winning portfolio websites

Mood:

* Emotional, nostalgic, cinematic, unforgettable
* Feels like a luxury digital scrapbook made by a world-class designer
* Every interaction should make the birthday feel special and memorable

Avoid:

* Generic gallery grids
* Template-looking designs
* Bright rainbow colors
* Dated UI patterns
* Anything that feels cheap or amateur
